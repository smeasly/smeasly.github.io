History of Web Design

I'm not a web-designer, nor do I intend to become one, but I do have an appreciation for the design trends of yesteryear.

I have made a very incomplete and perhaps very inaccurate tour through the 'history' of web design by creating my own interpretations of different eras of design. In fact, this page is my take on the modern design that we see today.

In honesty, I'm not too passionate about it and I think this shows. The old stuff on the other hand? Can't get enough.

Neoscape

This is the emulated browser! It's not much of an emulation but it is somewhat functional.
This is built in CSS, HTML, and uses very minimal Javascript. You can navigate around like it's 1999!
( As long as it's a page somewhere within this domain... )

I recommend turning on fullscreen for better effect! :).
