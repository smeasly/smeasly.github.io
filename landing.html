How to use your Neoscape Navigator

The main controls are in the Ribbon Bar, just above the address bar, and the Window Controls, at the top right of your screen.

Navigation

The Back button will return you to the page you just jumped from,
and the Forward button will take you to the page you last returned from.

Reload

If the page is not loading properly, use this to refresh the current page.

Home

This will take you back to the future, should you really wish to return.

Window Buttons (🗕 🗗 🗙)

In the top right corner, clicking any of these will let you view the page in a modern browser.
(This may be necessary to view linked external pages!)

About Neoscape Navigator

This is a crude emulation of the original Netscape Navigator by Mozilla, a very popular web-browser that was eventually discontinued due to becoming economically infeasible to continue development. Netscape was originally paid software, but when Microsoft started bundling Internet Explorer with copies of windows, it ended up completely undercutting Netscape's market share.

Most of this UI was based off of netscape.html - A simple netscape UI in HTML by opaeoh. Though I made many modifications, especially in making the UI somewhat functional.
